Tuesdays at Six

A Second-Chance Friends-to-Lovers Romance

Emma, a widowed engineer, returns to her late father's horse farm in northern England and reconnects with Luke, her first love and local farrier. Their structured friends-with-benefits arrangement on 'Tuesdays at six' evolves into genuine intimacy as they navigate grief, homecoming, and rekindled first love within a close-knit rural community. A high-heat, slow-burn reunion romance set against the backdrop of working farm life and emotional healing.

Your book aligns strongly with readers in the 'High-Heat Contemporary Romance' segment. Your manuscript scored 8.9/10 on engagement potential in this cluster, reflecting strong alignment with their tastes. Hallmarks of this segment include slow-burn pacing with high-heat content, second-chance romance themes, rural or small-town settings, and mature protagonists dealing with real-life challenges. Tuesdays at Six delivers all of these, from Emma's grief journey to the structured intimacy of the 'Tuesdays at six' arrangement.
