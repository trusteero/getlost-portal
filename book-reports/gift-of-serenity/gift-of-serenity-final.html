A Gift of Serenity by John Ellis

A Literary Psychological Thriller of Memory and Redemption

On a remote English island, a man haunted by memory loss and guilt sets out on a walking pilgrimage, hoping to find peace while the past stalks close behind. Caught between psychological suspense, romance, and the search for healing, his journey weaves locked-room mystery tension with the intimate pulse of redemption and love. Both a gripping thriller and a layered meditation on grief, resilience, and second chances, this is a story for readers who crave suspense laced with emotional depth.

Marketing Strategy

Pre-Launch

6 weeks pre-launch (awareness)

  • Introduce the book to wide audiences with emotional hooks and thematic intrigue.
  • Video teasers, carousel ads, short-form TikTok/Instagram reels
  • Maximise reach and engagement through interest-based targeting.

Launch

2 weeks around launch (conversion)

  • Encourage deeper exploration of the book's themes and author brand.
  • Long-form Facebook ads, Instagram stories, author interviews, podcast guest spots
  • Retarget engaged audiences with layered creative assets.

Post-Launch

6 weeks post-launch (sustain momentum)

  • Drive direct sales through strong calls to action and urgency messaging.
  • Amazon sponsored product ads, BookBub placements, email sequences
  • Prioritise low-cost, high-ROI conversions and pre-order pushes.
